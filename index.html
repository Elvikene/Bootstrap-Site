Peet Ruut Restaurant

Restaurant Peet Ruut seats 70 guests through three floors. Our restaurant is located in a dignified Old Town building with an exciting history.

The Ground floor is organised in two dining rooms: First room next to the Rataskaevu street can seat 16 persons and back room 18 persons There are large windows in both rooms. The tables can seat from 2 to 8 guests.

The Ground Floor area seats up to 24 guests among 6 tables. The room is perfect for intimate dinners and for long table events.

The dining room has capacity for 12 guests with a private hallway and wardrobe. The room is perfect for celebrations in smaller groups.

In the summer the restaurant has two summer terraces: -One in front of the house on Rataskaevu Street for enjoying the view of St.Nicholas Church. Street terrace can seat up to 18 persons. - Inner courtyard terrace is located behind our restaurant rooms with a lovely decor between historical Old Town walls. Courtyard can fit up to 22 persons. We can host various events from small  intimate candlelit dinners to larger private and corporate events and parties

Our Cheff Peet Ruut

Enjoy the finest British cuisine cooked by our Executive Chef Peet Ruut.

Our talented chef Chris Prow began his career almost 30 years ago, seeing him work around the world from South Africa, Barbados to Scotland. His successes have come one after another, being recognized by his peers he was awarded a Michelin plate back in 2017. Bringing all of this experience and knowledge to The Capital.

His vision for the menu is enhancing classic British dishes to a fine dining level, in line with The Capital’s legacy of providing a dining experience beyond to remember.

  

Chris is aiming to always introduce new and exciting dishes for his guests that reflects his style of cooking and the season.
